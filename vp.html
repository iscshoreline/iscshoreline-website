Message from ISC's Vice President!

Patricia Abby

Vice President of ISC 2023-2024

“Deciding to pursue my higher education through SCC is a choice filled with no regrets. Welcomed by the Indonesian communtuy at SCC, I felt the warm embrace of my culture, which ignited my commitment to its nurturing. Now writing as the vice president, I look back seeing how far we've come, how we stood by one another through hardships and laughters. Every one of us, regardless of our roles, has contributed far beyond the expected, working tirelessly to create an environment where everyone feels supported and valued. As I reflect on these experiences, my heart is full of gratitude for ISC, for providing a space that feels like a home away from home, for the unforgettable memories, and for the life-changing opportunities it has presented. This journey has taught us the true meaning of unity and resilience, proving that together, we can achieve extraordinary things. Thank you, ISC, for the friendships, lessons, and memories!”
