Message from ISC's Vice President!

Grace Salim

Vice President of ISC 2024-2025

“Traveling hundreds of miles from home, there’s always a lingering sense of longing—a quiet ache for the familiarity and warmth that home provides. The farther I am, the stronger the desire grows to be part of something that overcomes the distance and makes the unfamiliar feel a little more like home. Being part of ISC—a community bound by culture, understanding, and love for Indonesia—that sense of hiraeth is somehow cured. So many memories have shaped my journey, from serving as Secretary to stepping into my role as Vice President. Some were filled with joy and laughter, others with challenges—but all have accompanied me for two years, shaping my experience and deepening my connection to ISC. These moments are not just mine to cherish; they are part of ISC’s story. The successes and turbulences we’ve shared are what make ISC, ISC—a place of strength, unity, and purpose. As I look back, I know the memories, friendships, and lessons I’ve gained will stay with me. Cheers to our past, present, and future with ISC!”
