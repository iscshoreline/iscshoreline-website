About Us

Vision

To create a dynamic and inclusive Indonesian student community in Shoreline Community college that empowers its members to reach their full potential, connect with their cultural heritage, and positively impact the world.

Mission

We strive to create a welcoming and inclusive environment where Indonesian students can connect with their peers, develop new skills, and make a positive impact in their community. By promoting cultural awareness and diversity, we hope to foster a sense of pride and belonging among Indonesian students, while also supporting their academic and personal growth.
